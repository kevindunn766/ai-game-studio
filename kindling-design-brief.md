"Okay back to the game design. Start with building out the level streaming mechanism. Review the level streaming system used in Chimera Drift. It is robust and we'll tuned. We will use this. Use the isometric camera and spring arm setup from Snake_3d. Bring with it the camera sizing mechanism that zooms out with the size of the player/objects as the player approaches things and the tilt-shift photography effects. But small changes and subtle listen closely. In this game the player's piece doesn't move. The world moves. It is like a treadmill. The world will scroll around the player not the other way around.
